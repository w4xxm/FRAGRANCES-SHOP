L'Interdit Eau de Parfum Rouge by Givenchy is an Oriental Floral fragrance for women. L'Interdit Eau de Parfum Rouge was launched in 2021. L'Interdit Eau de Parfum Rouge was created by Dominique Ropion, Anne Flipo and Fanny Bal. Top notes are Ginger and Blood Orange; the middle notes are Tuberose, Jasmine and Chili Leaf; the base notes are Sandalwood, Patchouli and Vetiver.
Ingredients
ALCOHOL, PARFUM (FRAGRANCE), AQUA (WATER), LINALOOL, LIMONENE, BUTYL METHOXYDIBENZOYLMETHANE, HYDROXYCITRONELLAL, GERANIOL, CITRONELLOL, BENZYL SALICYLATE, COUMARIN, ALPHA-ISOMETHYL IONONE, EUGENOL, CITRAL.
